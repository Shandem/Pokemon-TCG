    How to play!

    Click Deal Cards to start the game. You will be given 3 cards at random.

    *Each card can only attack the card across from it. After each round, that card for YOU and the NPC are swapped out for new ones.
    *Click Deal Cards after each choice to get your new cards.

    The Goal: 1st to 5 points wins. *Tip* Force a tie fight, it gives no points BUT it will let you swap out that card for new one!
